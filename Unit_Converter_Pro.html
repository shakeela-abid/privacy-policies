Privacy Policy for Unit Converter Pro

Last updated: January 7, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Unit Converter Pro ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application Unit Converter Pro (the "App"). By using our App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information We DO NOT Collect

Unit Converter Pro is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, including:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Your conversion calculations or inputs

2.2 Local Data Storage

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your favorite conversions (stored locally for quick access)
  • App preferences and settings
  • Currency exchange rate cache (for offline functionality)

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 Currency Exchange Rates

Our App fetches live currency exchange rates from external APIs. When requesting currency rates:

  • No personal information is sent with these requests
  • Only the currency codes are transmitted
  • We cache rates locally to minimize external requests

3.2 Advertising

Our App may display advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data according to their privacy policy. We recommend reviewing Google's privacy policy at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings.

4. Data Security

Since we don't collect personal data, there's minimal risk to your privacy. All local data is stored securely on your device using standard encryption methods provided by the operating system.

5. Children's Privacy

Our App is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since we don't collect personal data from any users, this policy applies to all age groups.

6. International Data Transfers

As we don't collect personal data, there are no international data transfers of your personal information. Currency rate requests are made to international APIs but contain no personal data.

7. Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, traditional data protection rights (access, rectification, erasure) don't apply. However, you can:

  • Clear your locally stored favorites through the app settings
  • Uninstall the app to remove all local data
  • Disable ads through your device's advertising settings

9. App Permissions

Our App may request the following permissions:

10. Data Retention

Since we don't collect personal data on our servers, there's no data retention policy for personal information. Local data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app.
